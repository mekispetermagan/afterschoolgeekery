Fortune teller
What is it about?

This is a simple fortune teller machine. Well... As the Paranormal Extension of Scratch is not ready yet, we will cheat, and provide random "yes" or "no" answers to the user, whatever they ask.

Here's the end result of the project work:

Step 1: Preparing the scene

This is a project without sprites. All we have is three background. One for the instructions, one for the answer "yes", and one for the wnswer "no". Say goodbye to Scratchie, and create the three backgrounds. Everyone can design them the way they like. Here we use a minimal approach:

Feel free to follow your creative ideas, and draw something more detailed and interesting.

Since the "random" block in Scratch only works with numbers, let's just say "yes" is 1 and "no" is 2.
