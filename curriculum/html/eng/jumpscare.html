Jump Scare
What is it about?

A Jump Scare is a TikTok trend with roots in classic horror movies. It typically involves

  • Displaying a calm screen to make the audience lower their guards
  • Suddenly switching to a close-up of a scary face
  • Accompanying it with a loud, screaming sound.
This project is a Scratch implementation of this meme.

Here's the end result of the project work:

Step 1: Preparing the sprite

Ask kids to chose the sprite they find the cutest. It's ok if some of them copy you in their choice, but encourage them to make their own choices. The sprite they choose should have a face.

Go to "Costumes", and duplicate the main costume of the chosen sprite. It is also a good idea to delete the rest to avoid confusions. In the duplicated costume, delete everything except the face. Then select and enlarge the face, so that it fillls the whole stage. Finally, alter the face so that it becomes scary. Kids usually love this part! :)

Step 3: Adding the scream

Now let's add the screaming sound. Select the "Sound" tab, and click the speaker icon to select a new sound. There are three nice sound samples: "Scream1" and "Scream2" in "Voice" and "Screech" in "Animal". These are near each other in "All". Choose whichever you like (or a fourth one; or kids may even record their own screams).
