Operating in Hungary, Slovakia, and Ukraine, we teach computer programming to underprivileged kids. It is a skill which, according to predictions, will be in the future as important as being able to write is now.

Today's children are typically screen addicts, but we can change their minds from useless screen use to useful screen use. It's surprisingly easy to motivate them by working on intuitive and spectacular projects that provide a feeling of success quickly: digital storytelling, game development, and robot building. We have also had good experiences with children who can barely read and write. Many of them experienced their first learning successes by programming and robotics.
